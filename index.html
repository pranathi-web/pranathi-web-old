What is Pranathi ?


Pranathi has always been the angel daughter of celebration at Jyothy. It’s a festival that we don’t miss out on each year. She made her debut in the year 2018 by being an intercollegiate event and created history for centuries to remember. The second iteration of Pranathi is about to commence. Make sure you don't miss out the grand celebration.

Who are we ?


Jyothy Institute of Technology is a college that magnifies the grandeur of any event. The entire fest is more like a family celebrating a town's festival altogether.

What we provide?


The Festival houses a wide range of events to participate and enthrall covering vivid and extensive creative cultural activities. An Electrifying experience to witness top famous bands playing and interacting with a much lively crowd.
